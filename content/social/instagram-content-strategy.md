# PitchRank Instagram Content Strategy

> Created: 2026-03-10

---

## Content Pillars

Every post should fall into one of these four pillars. Aim for a balanced mix.

### Pillar 1: Rankings & Data (40% of content)
The core product. Stats-forward, visually bold, designed to stop the scroll.

**Post types:**
- Weekly Top 25 (national, by age group)
- Biggest Movers (climbers + fallers)
- PowerScore spotlight (what does 0.92 mean?)
- State-level leaderboards
- "By the Numbers" weekly recap
- Head-to-head matchup stats

**Visual style:** Dark green backgrounds, large yellow numbers, JetBrains Mono for stats

---

### Pillar 2: Team Spotlights & Community (25% of content)
Build relationships with clubs, parents, and players. This is what gets shared.

**Post types:**
- Team of the Week (highest PowerScore gain)
- Club profiles (history, notable results)
- "Who's #1?" age group reveals
- User-submitted team photos (with ranking overlay)
- Tournament recap graphics
- Shoutouts to teams that engage

**Visual style:** Photo-based with green overlay, team branding featured

---

### Pillar 3: Educational & Explainer (20% of content)
Build trust by showing how the algorithm works. Parents love transparency.

**Post types:**
- "How PitchRank Works" carousel series
- "What is Strength of Schedule?" explainer
- "Why Your Team Dropped" common reasons
- "3 Ways to Climb the Rankings" tips
- Myth busters ("Do blowouts matter?")
- Glossary terms (PowerScore, SOS, v53e layers)

**Visual style:** White/light backgrounds, numbered steps, clean typography

---

### Pillar 4: Engagement & Culture (15% of content)
Drive interaction, build community, and humanize the brand.

**Post types:**
- Polls: "Who wins this matchup?" (Story)
- "Guess the Rank" game
- This or That: ECNL vs GA, East vs West
- Meme/humor posts about soccer parent life
- Motivational quotes with data twist
- Behind-the-scenes (algorithm updates, new features)

**Visual style:** Mixed — fun, lighter tone, interactive elements

---

## Posting Schedule

### Frequency: 4-5 feed posts per week + daily Stories

| Day | Feed Post | Story |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| **Monday** | 🔥 Weekly Movers (carousel) | "Rankings just updated!" countdown |
| **Tuesday** | 📊 Top 25 by Age Group | Poll: "Biggest surprise this week?" |
| **Wednesday** | ⭐ Team Spotlight | Behind-the-scenes or fun fact |
| **Thursday** | 💡 Educational carousel | Q&A sticker: "Ask us anything" |
| **Friday** | — (rest or bonus post) | Weekend tournament preview |
| **Saturday** | 🏆 Weekend Matchup Preview | Live tournament coverage if possible |
| **Sunday** | — (rest) | "What games are you watching?" engagement |

### Optimal Posting Times (Youth Soccer Audience)
- **Weekdays**: 7:00-8:00 AM (parents' commute) or 8:00-9:00 PM (after practice)
- **Weekends**: 9:00-10:00 AM (before games) or 7:00-8:00 PM (post-game wind-down)
- Adjust based on Instagram Insights after 2-4 weeks of data

### Stories Strategy
- Post 3-5 Stories per day on active days
- Use interactive stickers (polls, quizzes, questions) 3x/week minimum
- Share any user tags/mentions to Stories immediately
- Use countdown stickers for Monday ranking drops

## Growth Tactics

### Hashtag Strategy
See `instagram-hashtag-strategy.md` for full breakdown.

### Engagement Rules
1. **Reply to every comment** within 2 hours during business hours
2. **Like and comment** on tagged posts from clubs/parents
3. **Follow** major clubs, leagues (ECNL, GA, MLS NEXT), and soccer media accounts
4. **Engage** with 10-15 accounts in the youth soccer space daily
5. **Reshare** user-generated content to Stories with a thank you

### Collaboration Opportunities
- **Club partnerships**: Offer to feature clubs that share PitchRank posts
- **Soccer parent influencers**: Partner with soccer mom/dad accounts
- **Tournament organizers**: Provide ranking graphics for their events
- **Youth soccer media**: Cross-promote with SoccerWire, TopDrawerSoccer, etc.

### Reels Strategy (Start Week 3+)
- 15-30 second format
- "Top 5 teams in [state]" countdown
- "This team jumped 3,000 spots — here's why"
- Screen recording of the rankings page with voiceover
- Trending audio + ranking reveals
