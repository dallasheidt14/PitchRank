## 3. Grid Aesthetic Strategy

### Visual Identity
- **Primary background**: `#0B5345` (Forest Green) — for data/stats posts
- **Secondary background**: `#052E27` (Dark Green) — for dramatic reveals
- **Accent pops**: `#F4D03F` (Electric Yellow) — headlines, highlights, numbers
- **Clean white**: `#FDFEFE` — for educational/explainer posts
- **Typography**: Oswald Bold for headlines, DM Sans for body, JetBrains Mono for stats

### Grid Pattern (3-column layout)
Alternate between post types to create visual rhythm:

```
[Stats/Dark] [Spotlight/Photo] [Educational/White]
[Carousel]   [Stats/Dark]      [Spotlight/Photo]
[Educational] [Carousel]       [Stats/Dark]
```

### Post Template Styles

**Style 1: "Stats Card" (Dark Green Background)**
- Background: `#0B5345` or `#052E27`
- Large stat number in `#F4D03F` (Oswald Bold, 72pt+)
- Supporting text in white (DM Sans)
- PitchRank logo watermark bottom-right
- Use for: Weekly rankings, PowerScore reveals, data points

**Style 2: "Spotlight" (Photo-based)**
- Action photo or team crest with green overlay
- Team name in Oswald Bold
- Stats bar at bottom with key metrics
- Use for: Team features, player highlights

**Style 3: "Educational" (White/Light Background)**
- Clean white background with green text
- Yellow accent bar or underline on key terms
- Step-by-step or numbered format
- Use for: How rankings work, tips, FAQs

**Style 4: "Carousel" (Multi-slide)**
- Slide 1: Hook/title (dark green, bold headline)
- Slides 2-8: Content (alternating dark/light)
- Final slide: CTA ("Link in bio", "Follow for more")
- Use for: Weekly movers, top 10 lists, deep dives
