## Post 10: MYTH BUSTER — "DO BLOWOUTS HELP?"
**Pillar:** Educational | **Format:** Single Image (1080×1080)

### Visual Description
- Clean white background
- Top: "MYTH vs FACT" in Oswald Bold, forest green
- Yellow divider line
- Left side (crossed out): "Winning 10-0 boosts your ranking more than 2-1"
- Right side (checkmark): "Goal difference is CAPPED at 6. Blowouts have diminishing returns."
- Bottom bar in forest green: "What matters more: beating a ranked team 2-1" in yellow
- PitchRank logo corner

### Caption
```
MYTH: "We need to run up the score to help our ranking."

FACT: Nope. Our algorithm caps goal difference at 6.

Beating a team 10-0 counts basically the same as 6-0 in our system. Here's why:

1️⃣ Blowouts usually mean a massive talent gap — they don't prove much
2️⃣ We care about competitive results against quality opponents
3️⃣ A 2-1 win against a top-25 team is worth WAY more than a 15-0 win against an unranked team

So if a coach is running up the score "for rankings"... that's not how it works.

What ACTUALLY helps:
✅ Playing strong opponents
✅ Winning close games against ranked teams
✅ Consistency over a full season
```
