### Option 1: Athletic Editorial
**File:** `option1-athletic-editorial.html`

**Aesthetic:** Sports Illustrated meets FiveThirtyEight
- **Typography:** Oswald (bold headers) + DM Sans (body) + JetBrains Mono (stats)
- **Colors:** Deep forest green (#0B5345) + electric yellow (#F4D03F) + white
- **Layout:** Magazine-style with dramatic headers, stats as hero elements
- **Details:** Diagonal cuts, rank badges with stars, grass texture overlay
- **Feel:** Authoritative, data-driven, professional but accessible

**Best for:** Parents who want serious rankings + Coaches who respect data + Admins who need credibility

---

### Option 2: Modern Pitch
**File:** `option2-modern-pitch.html`

**Aesthetic:** Nike/Adidas digital experience
- **Typography:** Poppins + Montserrat (geometric sans)
- **Colors:** Dark navy (#0A1929) + electric cyan (#00E5FF) + vibrant green (#4CAF50) + neon pink accents
- **Layout:** Asymmetric grid, overlapping elements, bold negative space
- **Details:** Hexagon patterns, animated gradients, stadium lights glow effects, glassmorphism
- **Feel:** Energetic, youthful, premium, modern

**Best for:** Tech-savvy parents + Younger coaches + Brand-conscious audiences

---

### Option 3: Youth Soccer Pride
**File:** `option3-youth-pride.html`

**Aesthetic:** Celebration-focused, warm and inviting
- **Typography:** Nunito (friendly rounded) + Playfair Display (elegant serif) + Quicksand
- **Colors:** Sunset orange (#FF8C42) + deep teal (#16697A) + gold (#FDB833) + cream (#FFF8E7)
- **Layout:** Card-heavy with generous spacing, soft shadows, rounded corners
- **Details:** Trophy emojis, floating decorations, medal gradients, celebration badges
- **Feel:** Warm, accessible, proud parent energy, emotionally inviting

**Best for:** Parents first (celebrates kids' achievements) + Family-oriented audience + Youth sports culture
