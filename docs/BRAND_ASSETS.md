## Design System

### Design Options (in `/design-mockups/`)

**Option 1: Athletic Editorial** ‚≠ê RECOMMENDED
- **Vibe:** Sports Illustrated meets FiveThirtyEight
- **Colors:** Deep forest green (#0B5345) + electric yellow (#F4D03F) + white
- **Fonts:** Oswald (headers) + DM Sans (body) + JetBrains Mono (stats)
- **Files:** `option1-athletic-editorial.html`, `option1-rankings-page.html`, `option1-team-profile.html`, `option1-compare-page.html`

**Option 2: Modern Pitch**
- **Vibe:** Nike/Adidas digital
- **Colors:** Dark navy (#0A1929) + electric cyan (#00E5FF) + neon green
- **File:** `option2-modern-pitch.html`

**Option 3: Youth Soccer Pride**
- **Vibe:** Warm, celebratory
- **Colors:** Sunset orange (#FF8C42) + deep teal (#16697A) + gold
- **File:** `option3-youth-pride.html`

## Usage Guidelines

### Do
- Use forest green as primary brand color
- Keep stats prominent and readable
- Use bold typography for impact
- Include the logo on all graphics

### Don't
- Use more than 3 colors per graphic
- Make text smaller than 14px on social
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Use colors outside the palette
