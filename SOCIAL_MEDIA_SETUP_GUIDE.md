### Negative Comments/Complaints

**If a team disagrees with their ranking:**
1. Acknowledge their concern professionally
2. Explain the methodology briefly
3. Invite them to learn more: "Check out our methodology page for details on how rankings are calculated"
4. Offer to discuss via DM or email
5. Never argue publicly

**Example Response:**
> "We understand your concern! Our rankings use cross-age power scoring based on game results. Each team's score is calculated using wins, losses, strength of schedule, and opponent quality. Learn more about our methodology at pitchrank.com/methodology. Feel free to DM us if you have specific questions! âš½"

### Data Errors

If you post incorrect information:
1. Delete the post immediately
2. Post correction with apology
3. Explain what happened (briefly)
4. Tag affected teams with correction

**Example:**
> "Correction: We previously posted incorrect rankings for U14 Girls. The correct top 5 is: [list]. We apologize for the error - a data sync issue has been resolved. Thanks for your patience! ğŸ™"

### Spam/Harassment

- Block spam accounts immediately
- Report harassment to platform
- Don't engage with trolls
- Create comment filters for profanity
