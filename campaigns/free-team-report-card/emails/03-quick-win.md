# Email 3: Quick Win / Value (Day 4)

**Subject:** How to read your team's PowerScore
**Preview:** It's not just a rank — here's what it measures

---

Your report card has a number on it called **PowerScore**.

Here's what it actually measures — and why it matters more than rank alone.

**PowerScore is a composite of:**

1. **Win quality** — beating a top-50 team counts more than beating a bottom-500 team
2. **Strength of schedule** — playing strong opponents raises your floor
3. **Recency** — last month's games matter more than September's
4. **Margin context** — a 3-0 win tells a different story than a 1-0 grind

A team ranked #50 with a 72 PowerScore playing ECNL is in a very different situation than a team ranked #50 with a 72 PowerScore in a state league.

**The quick way to use it:**

- **PowerScore going up + rank going up** = your team is genuinely improving
- **PowerScore going up + rank flat** = everyone around you is also improving (tough cohort)
- **PowerScore flat + rank dropping** = new teams entering the system above you

This is the kind of analysis that takes 30 seconds on PitchRank and would take hours doing manually.
