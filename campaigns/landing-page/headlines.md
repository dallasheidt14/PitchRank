# Landing Page Headline Variants

## QUICK PICK

**"You spend $10K a year on club soccer. Do you know where your team actually stands?"**
-> Best for: Solution-Aware parents who've been checking GotSoccer
-> Why: Validates their investment, asks the question they're already asking. Specific dollar amount triggers recognition.

## All Variants

1. **"You spend $10K a year on club soccer. Do you know where your team actually stands?"** (recommended)
   -> Question + Specificity. Hooks Optimizer and Investor archetypes.

2. **"25,000+ teams. 13-layer algorithm. Updated every week. See where yours ranks."**
   -> Direct Benefit + Mechanism. Leads with proof of scale.

3. **"GotSoccer ranks teams by how many tournaments they enter. We rank them by how they play."**
   -> Contrarian / Enemy. Directly attacks the villain.

4. **"The rankings parents have been asking for since YSR shut down."**
   -> Story / Nostalgia. Taps into the void. Strong with forum parents.

5. **"ECNL or MLS NEXT? Finally, a way to compare."**
   -> Curiosity + Unique Mechanism. Leads with the #1 unanswered question.

6. **"They said youth soccer rankings were broken. So we built new ones."**
   -> Story (Caples pattern). Setup + implicit resolution.

7. **"Rankings built from real game data -- not tournament politics."**
   -> Direct Benefit + Contrast. Clean, sharp, immediately understood.

8. **"One algorithm. Every league. Every age group. Real answers."**
   -> Specificity + Transformation. Broad scope, simple promise.

## A/B Test Recommendations

Test 1: Headline -- #1 (Question) vs #3 (Contrarian)
Why: Both target Solution-Aware parents but with different emotional triggers (validation vs frustration)
Metric: CTR from ad to landing page, scroll depth
Impact: +15-30% CTR
Priority: HIGH

Test 2: Headline -- #1 (Question) vs #7 (Direct Benefit)
Why: Question forces self-reflection; Direct Benefit states the value. Which resonates more?
Metric: Time on page, CTA clicks
Impact: +10-20% engagement
Priority: HIGH

Test 3: CTA -- "See Your Team's Ranking" vs "Find Your Team"
Why: First is benefit-oriented; second is action-oriented. Test which drives more clicks.
Metric: CTA click rate
Impact: +5-15% clicks
Priority: MEDIUM
