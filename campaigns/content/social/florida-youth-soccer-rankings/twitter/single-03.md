# Single Tweet: Right Team for Your Kid

The best team for your kid isn't always the highest-ranked one.

It's the one where they:
- Get 30+ minutes per game
- Face the right level of competition
- Actually enjoy playing

A top-ranked bench is worse than a mid-ranked starting spot.
