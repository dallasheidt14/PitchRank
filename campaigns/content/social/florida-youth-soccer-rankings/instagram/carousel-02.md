## Slide 2
**Headline:** 1. HOW DO RANKINGS TREND?
**Body:** Ask: "How have your teams' rankings trended over the past 2 years?"

Upward = good coaching.
Flat or declining = stagnation.
**Visual:** Trend line graphic going up

## Slide 3
**Headline:** 2. WHAT'S THE STRENGTH OF SCHEDULE?
**Body:** Are they playing real competition or scheduling easy wins to pad records?

Rankings adjust for opponent quality. You can't fake strength of schedule.
**Visual:** Two teams facing off graphic

## Slide 4
**Headline:** 3. HOW MUCH PLAYING TIME?
**Body:** The best team is where your kid plays 30+ minutes per game.

A top-ranked bench is worse than a mid-ranked starting spot.
**Visual:** Clock icon + "30+ min" in large text

## Slide 5
**Headline:** 4. WHERE DO PLAYERS GO NEXT?
**Body:** Ask: "How many players from this age group moved to ECNL or MLS NEXT?"

Player progression > team ranking.
**Visual:** Upward arrow with league logos

**Headline:** 5. WHAT'S THE REAL COST?
**Body:** Club dues + travel + tournaments + gear = $3K-$20K/year in Florida.

Rankings help you see if that investment matches the competitive level.
