## Primary Angle (Recommended)

**Know where your team really stands.**

Rankings built from real game data — 25K+ teams, 13-layer algorithm (v53e + ML), updated weekly. No politics, no guesswork. For parents and coaches who want one clear answer: *How good is our team?*

**Psychology:** Parents and coaches are tired of word-of-mouth and opaque league rankings. They want a single, defensible number and the ability to compare. Trust comes from transparency (we show the method) and scale (we cover the landscape).

**Headline direction:** "See where your team stands." / "Rankings you can trust." / "Real games. Real data. Real rankings."

**Best for:** Primary positioning across site, ads, and content. Works for both problem-aware and solution-aware audiences.

## Alternative Angles

### ① Data you can trust
**Statement:** The only youth soccer rankings powered by a transparent, multi-layer algorithm and real game results — not opinions or politics.

**Psychology:** Skepticism about "rigged" or biased rankings. Mechanism (v53e + ML, 365-day window, merge resolution) answers "why should I believe this?"

**Headline:** "Rankings based on data, not politics."

**Best for:** Parents/coaches who've been burned by unclear or biased rankings.

**Statement:** Fair rankings — same 13-layer process for every team, from rec to elite. No favoritism, no hidden adjustments.

**Best for:** Trust-building and differentiation when competitors are seen as opaque or unfair.

## Market Assessment

- **Sophistication:** Stage 2–3 (growing awareness, some alternatives like GotSport rankings, manual tracking, word of mouth).
- **Transformation:** From "I don't really know where we stand" to "I have a clear, defensible ranking and can compare."
- **Mechanism:** Proprietary 13-layer algorithm (v53e + ML Layer 13), 365-day game window, merge resolution, 25K+ teams; weekly updates.
- **Primary alternative:** League or provider rankings (GotSport, etc.), word of mouth, no formal ranking.

---

## Competitive Landscape (Summary)

- **Saturated claims:** "Best rankings," "accurate," "for youth soccer."
- **White space:** Transparency of method (layers, data window, ML), scale (25K+ teams), and "for parents and coaches" as a clear audience.
- **Differentiation:** Lead with mechanism (v53e, ML) and transparency; avoid generic "best" or "accurate" without proof.
