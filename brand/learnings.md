### 2026-03-30 — Headline preferences (landing page)
- Preferred **Direct Benefit + Mechanism** ("25,000+ teams. 13-layer algorithm. Updated every week. See where yours ranks.") and **Contrarian/Enemy** ("GotSoccer ranks teams by how many tournaments they enter. We rank them by how they play.")
- These beat Question, Story, Nostalgia, and Curiosity variants
- Takeaway: Lead with hard numbers and direct competitive contrast. Dallas gravitates toward confident, proof-heavy copy over emotional hooks or questions. Use mechanism language (layers, teams, weekly) and villain framing (GotSoccer) in future headlines.
